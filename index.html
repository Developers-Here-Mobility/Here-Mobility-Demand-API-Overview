SupplierRideStatus.RideStatus

RideStatus represents the different statuses a ride can be at

NameNumberDescription
UNKNOWN 0

Status is unknown
SUPPLIER_CONFIRMED 1

Supplier confirmed the ride, but driver is not yet allocated
DRIVER_ALLOCATED 2

Driver is allocated, but ride has not yet started
DRIVER_EN_ROUTE 3

Drive started and driver is en-route
DRIVER_POINT_OF_BOOKING 4

Driver is arrived at the pickup point, but passenger is not yet on board
PASSANGER_ON_BOARD 5

Passenger is on board and drive to destination starterd
DONE 6

Ride complete and passenger disembarked
CANCELED 7

Ride is cancelled (due to supplier or due to demander)
